A story steeped in emotions linked by deep memories.

A path born from the exchange and sharing of knowledge.

Caffè Santa Cruz comes to life from the entrepreneurial vision of Domenico Sileo, from the quality of the raw materials and from the attention to the blending and roasting process for a full-bodied coffee with an intense and persistent aroma.

Today Massimo continues the path taken by his father, accompanied by his sons Domenico and Marco with passion and respect for tradition.

Deep roots, growth, evolution for the creation of a unique product linked to the past but with constant observation of the future.
